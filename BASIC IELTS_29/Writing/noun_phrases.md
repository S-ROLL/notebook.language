## Clause
``S + V + (O)``
- Verb:
  - Intransitive $\rightarrow$ ``SV`` (E.g. Work).
  - Transitive $\rightarrow$ ``SVO`` (E.g. Do).
## N-phrases
- **Pre-modifier**:
  - ``ADJ + N`` $\rightarrow$ Characteristic.
  - ``N + N`` $\rightarrow$ Featuring/Platform.
- **Post-modifier**:
  - ``N + Ving/V3ed`` $\rightarrow$ Reduced form of relative clause.
